# Privacy Policy for Talkify

**Last Updated**: March 12, 2026

Talkify ("we", "our", or "us") operates the Talkify mobile application (the "App"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and protect your information when you use our application.

By using Talkify, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We collect certain information to provide, maintain, and improve the functionality of Talkify.

### Account Information

When creating an account, users may register using:

- Sign in with Apple
- Email and password

We may collect the following information:

- Name
- Email address
- Profile photo
- Authentication information

This information is used to create and manage your Talkify account and enable access to the app's features.

### User Content

Talkify allows users to upload or provide content for processing. This content may include:

- PDFs
- Images
- Books or documents
- Web links
- Text input
- Audio recordings

Content may be stored:

- On your device
- In secure cloud storage used by Talkify

This content is used solely to provide core features of the app such as audio narration, summaries, and AI-generated outputs.

## 2. AI Processing and Content Analysis

Talkify uses artificial intelligence technologies to process user-provided content.

Processing may involve:

- OpenAI services
- Apple Intelligence

When users request AI-powered features, relevant content may be temporarily transmitted to these services for processing.

**Important safeguards:**

- Content is processed only to generate requested outputs.
- Content is not used by Talkify to personally identify users.
- Talkify does not use user content to train its own AI models.

Third-party providers such as OpenAI or Apple may process data according to their own privacy policies.

## 3. User Content Ownership

Users retain ownership of any content they upload or submit to Talkify.

By uploading content, users grant Talkify a limited license to process the content solely for the purpose of providing app functionality.

Talkify does not claim ownership of user content.

Users are responsible for ensuring they have the legal rights to upload or process any material submitted through the app.

## 4. Analytics and Usage Data

To improve the performance and usability of Talkify, we collect analytics and diagnostic information.

This may include:

- App interactions
- Feature usage
- Session length
- Device type
- Operating system
- App version
- Crash reports
- Performance data

**Analytics tools used include:**

- Mixpanel
- Apple Analytics

These services help us understand how users interact with Talkify so we can improve the product.

Analytics data is aggregated and does not personally identify individual users.

## 5. Device Information

We may collect limited device information including:

- Device model
- Operating system version
- App version
- Anonymous device identifiers
- Crash logs

This information helps maintain app reliability and improve technical performance.

## 6. Subscriptions and Payments

Talkify provides subscription-based access to certain features.

All payments are processed securely through Apple's App Store payment system.

Talkify does not collect or store payment card details.

Subscription management, billing, and cancellations are handled through the Apple App Store.

## 7. Data Storage and Security

User data may be stored:

- On the user's device
- On secure cloud servers used by Talkify

We implement reasonable technical and administrative safeguards to protect data, including:

- Secure data transmission
- Encryption where appropriate
- Restricted access controls

Despite these safeguards, no system can guarantee absolute security.

## 8. Temporary Processing of Files

Some files uploaded to Talkify may be temporarily processed by cloud services or AI providers in order to generate requested outputs.

Files may be temporarily stored during processing and may be removed when processing is complete or when the user deletes them.

## 9. Data Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary to:

- Provide Talkify services
- Maintain user accounts
- Improve application functionality
- Comply with legal obligations

Users may delete their data at any time by deleting their account.

## 10. Deleting Your Data

Users can delete their account and associated data directly within the Talkify app.

**To delete your account:**

1. Open the Talkify app
2. Navigate to Account Settings
3. Select Delete Account

**Once an account is deleted:**

- Account information will be removed
- Associated user data will be deleted from Talkify systems where feasible

Some limited information may be retained where necessary for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or system integrity.

These services may collect information according to their own privacy policies.

We encourage users to review those policies for more information.

We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If such information is discovered, we will take steps to delete it promptly.
